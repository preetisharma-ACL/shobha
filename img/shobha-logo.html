About Developer

Sobha Realty is a leading real estate developer in India, known for its high-quality residential and commercial projects. Founded in 1995 by P.N.C. Menon, the company has built a strong reputation for delivering luxurious and meticulously designed properties across major cities like Bangalore, Chennai, Pune, and Gurugram. Sobha Realty follows a unique backward integration model, ensuring in-house expertise in construction, architecture, and interior design, which guarantees superior quality and timely delivery. With a commitment to sustainability and innovation, Sobha continues to redefine urban living, offering world-class amenities and elegant designs that cater to modern lifestyles.
